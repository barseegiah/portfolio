About Me

Giahseymehn Barseegiah
Our President

Mr. Giahseymehn Barseegiah is the man behind the scenes of our company that uses his knowledge and experience in technology to bring our creative visions (and those of our customers) to life. Not only is he an IT Specialist, he’s also an Entrepreneur, Poet, Songwriter, Digital Activist and Student Leader. He’s a citizen and resident of Liberia, a country located in West Africa. He’s currently the Innovation Director of the Liberia Information Technology Student Union (LITSU), an organization that seeks out and enlists students with an interest in Technology to broaden and sharpen their technical skills. He’s also a writer for “All-Factz” and “Techxy.” He holds a Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) with a concentration in System Administration. To note the extent of his technical skills even more, he holds multiple professional certificates relating to Information Technology (IT) and the Leadership field. Fun Fact: He’s the CEO of “Spark-Shooters Records,” a label specializing in Music and Acting.
